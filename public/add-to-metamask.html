Add Your NFT to MetaMask

Note: Make sure you have MetaMask installed and are connected to the Sepolia Test Network.

Follow these steps:

  1. Open your MetaMask wallet by clicking the extension icon
  2. Click on the "NFTs" tab at the bottom
  3. Click on "Import NFT" button
  4. Enter the NFT contract address:
    0x5FbDB2315678afecb367f032d93F642f64180aa3
  5. Enter the Token ID:
    1
  6. Click "Import" to add the NFT to your wallet

Connect to Sepolia Test Network

If you don't see the Sepolia Test Network in MetaMask:

  1. Click on the network dropdown at the top of MetaMask
  2. Click "Add Network"
  3. Go to "Networks" > "Add Network" > "Add Network Manually"
  4. Enter the following details:
    • Network Name: Sepolia Test Network
    • New RPC URL: https://eth-sepolia.public.blastapi.io
    • Chain ID: 11155111
    • Currency Symbol: ETH
    • Block Explorer URL: https://sepolia.etherscan.io
  5. Click "Save"
